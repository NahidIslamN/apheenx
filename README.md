About This System

This is a robust e-commerce and video content platform built with Django.

The system is designed to support both product selling and video content streaming in a secure and scalable way.

E-commerce Features

Users can browse and purchase products
Orders are tracked with full details and pricing
Supports categories, discounts, and stock management


Video Platform Features

Supports video content with trailers and full videos
Users can subscribe or pay to access premium video content
Video views are tracked for analytics and monitoring


Media Security System

Strong protection on all video content
Full videos are not publicly accessible
Only trailers and preview content are publicly visible
Paid users can access full video content after verification
Media files are securely stored and accessed only through the system, not direct public URLs
Prevents unauthorized access to sensitive video resources


Summary

This system ensures a secure media streaming experience combined with e-commerce functionality, where content protection and controlled access are a top priority.
